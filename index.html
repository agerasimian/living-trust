California Living Trust Builder

Fill in the form → a ready-to-review trust appears on the right → print or save as PDF. Everything stays in your browser.

This is a self-help template, not legal advice, and not a law firm. Using it creates no attorney–client relationship. It is built for California only and for straightforward estates. Please have a California estate attorney review it before signing — especially if anyone has special needs, you have a blended family, a taxable estate (over ~$15M), or business interests. A trust does nothing until it is signed & notarized and funded (assets retitled into it).
